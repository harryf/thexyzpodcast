This week the boys take on a serious and difficult subject - roofies aka Rohypnol aka Flunitrazepam and similar drugs. Its become way too common for people to get unknowingly drugged at bars and clubs to the point where it's considered almost "normal" ... just a risk we have to take going out. This shit has to stop!

Warning: if you've had bad experiences with people putting drugs like roofies in your drink or similar traumatic experiences, this may be a triggering episode to listen to - suggest skip it.
