This podcast is to honor the memory of Mr Bryan Vokey ( @mybryanvokey ) who's many years of service to comedy - including touring in Switzerland - tragically ended on this podcast episode. Bryan will be remembered for his work on The Worst Hour Podcast and the The Fat Jocks Podcast, as well as his numerous careers in a punk rock band, as a preschool teacher, doing top-secret work for the NSA and as a stand-up comedian. Find out more about Bryan on this special website dedicated to his memory - https://www.bryanvokey.com
