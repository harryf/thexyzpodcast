21. May 2021... the boys sold out their first live show at ComedyHaus in Zürich... an evening of comedy then this podcast involving the audience. It was heaps of fun - at the end that special vibe you get after a great show. Whether it's fun to listen to, we don't know but hopefully next time, you'll join us in person. Thank you so much to everyone that came out and supported. We'll do it again for sure...
