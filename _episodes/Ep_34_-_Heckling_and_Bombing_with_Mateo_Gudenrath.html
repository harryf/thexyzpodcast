This week the boys are taken on a wild ride by friend and special guest Mateo Gudenrath ( @mateocomedy ) - winner of the Swiss Comedy Talent Award 2020. We learn about how to bomb on stage while fighting off angry hecklers. Mateo can also be found speaking German on the Landert & Gudenrath Podcast and the Endstation Podcast.
