This week the boys catch up with comedy buddy Jack Roberts, the sexiest Mancunian comedian living in Switzerland... in his age bracket... that also teaches. Among other things, they discuss the most controversial comedy show of the past two years of English standup in Zürich... The Originals.

Catch Jack performing at shows in an around Zürich and check out his Instagram @jackschweiz.
