This week the boys gather - after months of beer and no exercise - to meet with Anne from Bubble Yoga. Anne tells the amazing tale of how she quit her career as an international marketing manager right at it's peak, left Australia and moved to Switzerland to become a Yoga Teacher. These days she teaches everyone from kids to bankers and even manages to get the boys a little motivated...
