This week the boys are joined by traveling comedienne Bylgja Babylons, who burst out of Icelands growing comedy scene, like a hot spring, to make it across some water to Edinburgh, where she now lives. Bylgja happened to be passing through Switzerland on the English Standup Comedy tour so dropped in on the XYZ boys.

Find out more about Bylgia on...

If you're at the Edinburgh Fringe this year make sure to check out Bylgja's show "Once Upon a Time in Iceland".
