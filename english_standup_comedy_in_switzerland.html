--- layout: episodes title: English Stand Up Comedy in Switzerland ---

Believe it or not, Switzerland has a rich and growing scene of events and clubs hosting stand up comedy in English. Most events are published on Facebook or Event Frog. Events take place in Zürich, Basel, Zug, Lausanne and other locations around Switzerland. Links to various comedy clubs are listed below...

Zürich

  • Comedy Nights Zürich

    Hosted by Ahmet Bilge, Comedy Nights Zürich hosts events at various bars including Kontiki in Niederdorf, The Alehouse near the University and the Trottoir Bar off Langstrasse. Check out the events here on Facebook.

  • English Stand-Up Comedy

    Hosted by Benjamin Delahaye, English Stand-Up runs events at ComedyHaus near Albisriederplatz. Check out the events here on Facebook.

  • Comedy Kiss

    Hosted by Shane Norton, Comedy Kiss runs events at Auer & Co. and Impact Hub Zürich - Viadukt. Check out the events here on Facebook.

Basel

  • On The Run Comedy Club

    Hosted by Teddy Hall, the On The Run Comedy Club in Basel runs events at Jukebox Basel. Events are publised on Facebook here.

  • Comedy Basel

    Hosted by Markus Seppälä, the Comedy Basel runs events at KLARA, The Lab and Don Camillo. Events are publised on Facebook here.

Zug

  • English Stand-Up Comedy in Zug

    Hosted by Andalee Bailey, English Stand-Up Comedy in Zug runs events at Theater Casino Zug. Events are publised on the Theater Casino website here.

Carouge (near Lausanne)

  • Expat Comedy Night

    Hosted by Jen Kirwin, Expat Comedy Night in Carouge (near Lausanne) runs events at Caustic Comedy Club. Events are publised on Facebook at the Expat Comedy Website.

Around Switzerland

  • The American Stand-Up Show

    Also hosted by Ahmet Bilge, The American Stand-Up Show runs events in Bern, Basel, Zürich and more. Check out the events here on Facebook.

  • International Comedy Club

    Promoted by Guy Stevens, The Internation Comedy Club brings international comedians to Switzerland. Check out upcoming events here on the website.
