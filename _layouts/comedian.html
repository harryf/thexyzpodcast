    "My name is actually Harry Fücks. It's a German name..."

    Harry Fücks is struggling with the trifecta of a midlife crisis, being a single dad and failing to live up to his own name... comedy is his coping strategy. Originally from the UK - with just hint of German - Harry has spent 20 years living in Switzerland and working in that most dangerous of professions - software development.

    I started doing stand-up comedy in 2018 and since 2022 have been averaging about 2-5 English shows a week, mostly in Switzerland for expat audiences, as well as Barcelona and Paris. I have around 40 minutes of solid material and I've performed for crowds up to ~400 people; most of the time for 30-50 people audiences. I also host my own open-mic IN YOUR FACE comedy. Have done / can also do comedy in German...
